Purpose & Guidelines

This system documents the ongoing professional development of the Simulation Operations Team. Maintaining regular continuing education is both a requirement for SSH Accreditation and a reflection of our commitment to excellence in simulation-based education.

Acceptable Activities

  • Professional podcasts, webinars, or online courses related to simulation, healthcare education, or operations
  • Books, journal articles, or trade publications focused on simulation, adult learning, or technology integration
  • Conference sessions, workshops, or local simulation community meetings
  • YouTube videos, vendor tutorials, or software trainings that enhance operational or technical knowledge

All activities should have a direct connection to healthcare simulation, education, or operations, and should contribute meaningfully to your professional growth within the field.
